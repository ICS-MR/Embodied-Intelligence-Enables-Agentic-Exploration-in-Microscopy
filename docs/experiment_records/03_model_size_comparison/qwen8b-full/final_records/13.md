## 4. Execution Result and Failure Reason Analysis

Execution result: The log shows the system first switched to 4×, but then used 20× DAPI to acquire a 5×1 mm area, attempted to move from an incorrect tumor JSON without performing organoid detection, and finally acquired a 3×3 mm area at 20×.

Failure reason analysis: The core low- to high-magnification workflow was not established. The 5×1 mm scanning phase should not have switched to 20×, and no organoid detection function was called, nor was `organoid_locations_list.json` generated or read. Subsequently, `tumor_locations_list.json` was loaded, and no corresponding detection output appeared in the log. The move to the “first detected region” lacked justification, so the final 3×3 mm high-magnification acquisition cannot be confirmed to be on the first organoid region. Furthermore, organoids are 3D targets, and the final 20× acquisition explicitly did not configure a Z-stack, which also fails to meet the requirements for 3D target acquisition.
