## 3. Expected Results

Expected to complete a 4× brightfield low-magnification scan of the organoid sample at the current position, record the detected organoid coordinates, and clearly localize the second organoid region. Subsequently, acquire images of the second organoid using blue and red fluorescence channels under the 20× objective. The acquisition process should include or configure the appropriate Z-axis range, and merge the blue and red channels using a suitable color mapping to generate a merged image.

## 4. Execution Results and Failure Cause Analysis

Execution results: Only 4x bright-field auto-brightness, autofocus, and bright-field image acquisition at the current position were completed; subsequent organoid detection recording, 20x blue/red fluorescence acquisition and merging for the second organoid were not actually completed.
Failure analysis: The image analysis step errored due to the use of a prohibited `open` operation, resulting in no usable organoid detection JSON; subsequently, the 20x step errored due to the use of a prohibited `locals` operation, failing to load the second organoid coordinates, move to that target, and acquire DAPI/TRITC images; the final merging step explicitly indicated that DAPI and TRITC images were missing and could not be merged. In addition, even if the subsequent acquisition code were executable, the final 20x fluorescence acquisition for the three-dimensional organoid target was not configured with Z-stack, nor was Z-stack reasonably considered.

Overall assessment: Failure
