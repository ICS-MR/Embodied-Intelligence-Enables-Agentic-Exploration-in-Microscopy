Failure analysis: The core low-to-high magnification workflow basically meets the task requirements, and automatic brightness and autofocus were performed before brightfield acquisition; however, organoids are three-dimensional targets, and the final 20× target acquisition stage clearly did not configure Z-stack, nor was a reasonable explanation given for acquiring only a single slice. Therefore, the high-magnification imaging insufficiently covers the three-dimensional structure, and cannot be judged as fully successful.

Overall assessment: Partial success
