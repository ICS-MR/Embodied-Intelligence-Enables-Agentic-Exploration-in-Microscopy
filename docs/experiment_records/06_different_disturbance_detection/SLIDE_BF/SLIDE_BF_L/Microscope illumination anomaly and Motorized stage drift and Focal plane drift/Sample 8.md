Relative to the initial reference state, a pronounced global displacement of the specimen towards the upper-left quadrant of the field of view was observed. Detected Anomalies

Four anomalies were identified:

Specimen translation — a global positional shift attributable to drift of the motorized stage.

Focal plane drift — manifesting as a pronounced defocus blur across the image.

Illumination anomaly — reduced and non-uniform illumination intensity relative to the reference state.

Specimen contamination — presence of a bright spot and fine dark particulates in the lower-right quadrant.
