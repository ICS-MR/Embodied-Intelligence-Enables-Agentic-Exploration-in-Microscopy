Organoids

## 3. Expected Results

It is expected to complete a 10× low-magnification brightfield scan of a 4×4 mm area of the organoid sample, identify organoid positions through image analysis, and record their coordinates. Subsequently, the first organoid coordinate should be loaded and the objective switched to 20×, acquire images in the blue and green fluorescence channels at that position, and configure or consider Z-axis acquisition for the three-dimensional organoid target.

## 4. Execution Result and Failure Cause Analysis

Execution Result: Only the automatic brightness, autofocus, and stitched acquisition of the 4000×4000 µm area at the current position under 10× brightfield mode were actually completed. The subsequent organoid coordinate recording and 20× blue/green fluorescence Z-stack acquisition of the first organoid were not successfully completed.
Failure analysis: The 10×, 4×4 mm brightfield scanning step essentially met the requirements, but the image analysis stage triggered an error due to the use of a prohibited `open` operation, and the specified `organoid_positions.json` was not reliably saved. At the same time, if the detection function itself saved a JSON, the filename might also be inconsistent with the subsequent `load_target_locations('organoid_positions.json')` call. In the final 20× stage, although an attempt was made to load the first target, move to it, and configure DAPI/FITC channels and Z-stack, due to a direction handling error in the range returned by `z_stack_range`, `set_z_stack` triggered "Z-axis step direction conflicts with start and end range" and aborted, failing to execute `run_acquisition` to successfully acquire blue and green fluorescence images of the first organoid. As a result, the core coordinate recording and the final 20× blue/green channel imaging output were missing.

Overall assessment: Failure
