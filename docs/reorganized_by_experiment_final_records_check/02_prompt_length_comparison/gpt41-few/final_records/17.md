## 3. Expected Results

It is expected to complete a 4× brightfield low-magnification scan of a 3×3 mm area of the organoid sample, identify organoids based on the scanned image, and record the target positions across all fields of view or tiles. Subsequently, under a 20× objective, DAPI, FITC, and TRITC fluorescence channel images should be acquired for each organoid at the recorded positions, with Z-stack acquisition reasonably included according to organoid thickness.

## 4. Execution Results and Failure Cause Analysis

Execution results: The 4× brightfield automatic brightness adjustment, focusing, Z-stack configuration, and large-area acquisition over a 3×3 mm region were actually completed, but organoid detection/saving and sequential acquisition of the three fluorescence channels at 20× were not executed successfully.

Failure cause analysis: The low-magnification 4× brightfield scanning generally met the task requirements and accounted for organoid Z-stacks; however, the image analysis stage failed due to the use of the prohibited `open`, resulting in no organoid position JSON being successfully generated or registered for subsequent loading. Subsequently, the 20× acquisition stage failed due to the use of the prohibited `locals`, failing to load target positions, not actually moving to each organoid, and not completing Z-stack acquisition in the DAPI, FITC, and TRITC channels. Therefore, the core latter half of the task is missing, and it can only be judged as a partial success.

Overall Judgment: Partial Success
