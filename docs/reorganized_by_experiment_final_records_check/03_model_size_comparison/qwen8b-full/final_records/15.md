## 2. Biological Samples Used

Organoids

## 3. Expected Results

It is expected to complete low-magnification brightfield imaging of a 2×2 mm array region of the organoid sample, and from the brightfield image, obtain and load the coordinates of each organoid via organoid localization analysis. Subsequently, switch to a 20× objective, acquire images at each organoid position in the order of blue, green, and red fluorescence channels, and configure or cover the necessary Z-axis range according to organoid thickness.

## 4. Execution Results and Failure Cause Analysis

Execution Results: The workflow completed 4× brightfield 2×2 mm area acquisition, organoid detection, and acquired DAPI/FITC/TRITC three-color fluorescence images of the first detected organoid at 20×.

Failure Cause Analysis: Low-magnification brightfield area acquisition and brightfield-based organoid localization basically met the requirements, but subsequently only target_bounding_boxes[0] was used to acquire the first organoid, without iterating through all detected organoids. Therefore, the core objective of "each organoid" was not completed. Additionally, since organoids are three-dimensional structures, the final 20× fluorescence acquisition was explicitly not configured with Z-stack, and no reasonable explanation for single-plane acquisition was provided, posing a risk of insufficient depth-of-focus coverage.

Overall Assessment: Partial Success
