## 4. Execution Results and Failure Cause Analysis

Execution results: The workflow configured the DAPI/blue fluorescence channel, 20x current objective, 24-well plate position, time series with 24 frames at one-hour intervals, organoid Z-stack parameters, and initiated full-well automatic acquisition.

Failure cause analysis: The core configurations of blue fluorescence, 24-well plate, 20x, 24-hour hourly acquisition, and Z-stack were basically covered; however, the code moved to the first well only after autofocus, and then called z_stack_range to recommend the Z-stack range without refocusing in the first well/representative well field of view, resulting in insufficient reliability of the organoid Z-stack range and subsequent long-term acquisition focal plane. The fixed 200 ms exposure, while acceptable as fluorescence exposure, is not truly optimized automatic exposure. Overall, the main workflow is executable but carries critical imaging quality risks.

Overall assessment: Partial success
