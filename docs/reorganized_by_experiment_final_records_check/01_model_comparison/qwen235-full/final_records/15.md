## 4. Execution Results and Failure Cause Analysis

Failure Cause Analysis: The core workflow essentially covered the task requirements, and the detection results were loaded and used for individual organoid acquisition; the Z-stack required for the 3D structure of organoids was also considered. However, at the high-magnification stage, autofocus was performed while still in the brightfield channel with brightness set to 0 and exposure still at the low brightfield exposure, and then the Z-stack range was recommended based on this unreliable state, which may lead to unreliable focus and Z-range for high-magnification fluorescence acquisition. Therefore, it cannot be judged as completely successful.

Overall Judgment: Partial Success
