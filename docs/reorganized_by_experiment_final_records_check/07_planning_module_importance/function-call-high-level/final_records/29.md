## 4. Execution Results and Failure Cause Analysis

This is below the exposure range typically required for fluorescence imaging, which may lead to insufficient DAPI signal, reduced autofocus reliability, and decreased accuracy of subsequent Cellpose counting.

Overall assessment: partially successful
